Nutrition

Nutritional requirement of organisms vary, for humans these requirements are molecules that provide energy and essential elements for surevival. The molecules are broken down into small absorbable bits through digestion.

Digestion is the chemical and mechanical breakdown of large edible organic molecules into smaller absorbable ones. Digestion in Humans starts in the mouth. Ingestd food is chewed and chemically broken down by enzymes into small folds called chyme. Chyme passes throgh the oesophagus by paristalysis into the stomach were temporary storage and further digestion takes place.

In the stomach, HCl pepsin, Renein and other gastro enzymes are released which chages the PH, disinfects the food and breaks down protein. The final product of this vigorous process chime is released into the duodenum of the small intestine
